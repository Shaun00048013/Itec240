Cooling

Gpus are usually the most expensive component in a computer . This is mainly due the processor having significantly more transistors than CPUS.

Since GPUs have a significant amount of transistors, this results in extreme amounts of heat being produced during usage . This has necessitated the need for cooling soloutions to be developed for Video Cards

Some video cards have blower style coolers which are usually just adequate enough

Many manufacturers have created their own proprietary open air coolers which offer more fans ,bigger heatsinks and beter airflow for improved cooling
