Also known as a display adapter or graphics card, a video card is an expansion card that connects to a computer motherboard .

It is used to create and output picture or rendered images on a display (eg monitor) Video cards are used to render games and 3d applications in place of integrated graphics as they are significantly more powerful due to them having increaed and more advanced compute capabilities and dedicated memory
